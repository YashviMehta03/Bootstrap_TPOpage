About Us


The TPO (Training and Placement Office) functions for connecting VJTI students and graduates with employers (Industry, Start-up , etc ) TPO facilitates students/graduates for requisite soft-skills as per industry standards. The office focuses on placements and internships, and is responsible for connecting VJTI students to the corporate world and the industry, and acts as a gateway.VJTI Mumbai has been most preferred academic institute of Maharashtra, to a large number of organisations for recruiting outstanding employees with immense future leaders.

Recruitment Process -


  • Placement process for a semester starts about two months ahead of the semester.
  • Companies provide necessary data through Job announcement forms.
  • Interested and eligible students apply
  • Then companies visit the campus during the placement season
  • The selection list is announced as soon as the company finalizes the same.
